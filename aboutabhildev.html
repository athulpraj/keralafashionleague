ABOUT ABHIL DEV

Abhil Dev is the founder of Kerala Fashion League and Proprietor of AbhilDev.com. Abhil was a Businessman before he was lured into the world of fashion. After having attended countless International fashion pageants, Abhil was keen on bringing this concept to his home State, and this is how the brand - Abhil Dev was conceived. He hopes to take the League to various states in the country. In addition, Abhil also initiated the KFL Rice Bucket Challenge, which is a noble has garnered him much praise and media coverage.
